Program name: Miles & More

LOT participates in and uses the Miles & More program (same as Lufthansa’s) for its loyalty / mileage scheme. 
LOT even highlights special offers like “Mileage Bargains”, extra baggage, lounge access, Fast Track, etc. depending on status. 
They also provide status-based perks (Frequent Traveller, Senator, HON) via Miles & More within LOT operations.
